JOLLY RANCHER, fruit-flavored hard candies


The Jolly Rancher Company was first founded in 1949, and their first store opened the same year in Colorado.

They offered a five-cent hot cinnamon taffy stick, and due to its popularity, expanded their hard candy offerings.

Jolly Rancher's original flavors were watermelon, apple, and blue raspberry. Current flavors include cherry, blue raspberry, grape, green apple, fruit punch and watermelon.

They are made from corn syrup, sucrose, glucose, or fructose syrup, and artificial flavoring and color is added later on. These are a great snack to share with friends.
