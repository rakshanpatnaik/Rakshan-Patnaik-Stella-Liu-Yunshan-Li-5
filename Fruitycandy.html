SKITTLES, multicolored, fruit-flavored, covered in a hard sugar shell


First produced by a British company in 1974, and introduced in the United States in 1982.

For a few years between 2013 to 2017, Skittles replaced the lime flavor with the green apple skittle, causing a lot of backlash.

In September 2021, they reverted back to their original lime skittle in their main Skittle packs.

Recently, Skittles introduced Skittles Gummies. So if chewy candy is more your style, Skittles has something for you, with all the same flavors! The other pack you can get is wild berry, which comes with Berry Punch, Melon Berry, Strawberry, Wild Cherry, and Rasberry.
